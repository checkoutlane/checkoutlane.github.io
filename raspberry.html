raspberry tart, juicy, sweet
The first time I encountered raspberry was in a jelly bean. On one of my first trips to the grocery store after I moved to the US, I followed my theory that red colored candy tend to taste good (except for Red Hots, never again). I had never heard of the fruit before. That day I fell down the rabbit hole of raspberry flavored foods: cookies, cakes, ice cream, anything. Raspberries are my answer to everything. Maybe it’s because I’ve rarely met a raspberry I didn’t like, so raspberries act as my safety net.
